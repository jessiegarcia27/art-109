A fire safety tool that safely guides children out of danger, providing interactive support and peace of mind for parents. 

Duration: 2 Months  

Children under 10 account for nearly 40% of fire-related fatalities in residential fires.

Exploration

It started as a wall plug-in, but safety concerns and the need for portability pushed me to rethink it. I turned it into a friendly, portable animal with a built-in nightlight and a charger that blends into its playful design.

BOOM ON SOCIAL MEDIA

To gauge public interest, I posted it on social media and received overwhelmingly positive feedback.

SafeBuddy works as a comforting nightlight during normal use and, in emergencies, displays LED arrows and plays parent-recorded messages to guide children safely. It wirelessly charges and connects to an app so parents can control settings, record messages, and monitor its location.
